Configure Your ChatGPT Prompt

How to Use:

  1. Enter the number of questions you want in the "Quantity" field.
  2. Type the theme of the interview questions in the "Theme" field (e.g., "Front-end development with React").
  3. Click the "Generate Prompt" button to create your custom prompt.
  4. Use the "Copy Code" button to copy the generated prompt.
  5. Paste the prompt into the ChatGPT input field and send it to receive your interview questions.
  6. Note: For correct operation, it is recommended to use ChatGPT 4.
